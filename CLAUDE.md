- Helping women feel confident and love their hair
- A judgment-free, welcoming space, ideal for first-timers
- Approachable luxury that won't break the bank
- Personalized and customized to each client ("let's create your look")
- Good vibes and a relaxing private-suite experience

## Writing Rules
- Conversational and direct, written in second person ("you")
- No em dashes
- No emojis in body copy; carry the upbeat, friendly energy through word choice instead
- Avoid AI filler: "in today's world," "look no further," "nestled in the heart of," "unlock," "elevate"
- Short, clean, easy-to-read sentences
